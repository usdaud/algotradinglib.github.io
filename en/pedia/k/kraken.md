# Kraken

Kraken is a prominent cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) that allows users to [trade](../t/trade.md) a variety of digital assets. Founded in 2011 by Jesse Powell, Kraken has established itself as one of the most reliable and secure platforms in the cryptocurrency space. Its headquarters are in San Francisco, California, and it is known for [offering](../o/offering.md) a broad [range](../r/range.md) of services, including spot trading, [futures](../f/futures.md) trading, staking, and [margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md).

### History and Founding

Kraken was founded in 2011, but it was officially launched in September 2013. Jesse Powell, the company's CEO, created Kraken in response to the [security](../s/security.md) challenges faced by other cryptocurrency exchanges at the time, particularly Mt. Gox, which later became infamous for its massive [security](../s/security.md) breach and subsequent [bankruptcy](../b/bankruptcy.md). Powell aimed to build an [exchange](../e/exchange.md) that emphasized [security](../s/security.md), [transparency](../t/transparency.md), and [customer](../c/customer.md) satisfaction.

### Security Features

One of Kraken's most significant selling points is its commitment to [security](../s/security.md). The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) has never been hacked, which sets it apart from many of its competitors. Kraken employs a broad [range](../r/range.md) of [security](../s/security.md) measures to protect its users' funds and personal information:

- **Cold Storage**: The majority of usersâ€™ funds are stored offline in cold storage environments, making them inaccessible to hackers.
- **Two-[Factor](../f/factor.md) Authentication (2FA)**: Users are encouraged to enable 2FA to add an extra layer of [security](../s/security.md) to their accounts.
- **PGP/GPG Email Encryption**: Kraken provides email encryption to protect sensitive communications between the platform and its users.
- **SSL Encryption**.

### Trading Options

Kraken offers various trading [options](../o/options.md) to accommodate different types of traders:

- **Spot Trading**: This is the straightforward buying and selling of cryptocurrencies at current [market](../m/market.md) prices. Kraken provides a user-friendly interface for spot trading, which is suitable for both beginners and experienced traders.
- **[Margin Trading](../m/margin_trading.md)**: Traders can [leverage](../l/leverage.md) their positions by borrowing funds to [trade](../t/trade.md) larger amounts than they currently [hold](../h/hold.md). Kraken offers margins up to 5x on certain trading pairs.
- **[Futures](../f/futures.md) Trading**: Kraken [Futures](../f/futures.md), a division launched in 2020, allows traders to buy and sell [futures contracts](../f/futures_contracts.md) on cryptocurrencies. This enables users to speculate on the future price movements of assets with [leverage](../l/leverage.md) up to 50x.
- **Staking**: Kraken offers staking services for several cryptocurrencies, allowing users to earn rewards by participating in the network's proof-of-stake consensus mechanism.

### Fees

Kraken's [fee](../f/fee.md) structure is tiered based on the user's [30-day trading volume](../1/30-day_trading_volume.md), rewarding higher [volume](../v/volume.md) traders with lower fees. The fees are as follows:

- **Spot Trading Fees**: Maker fees [range](../r/range.md) from 0.00% to 0.16%, and taker fees [range](../r/range.md) from 0.10% to 0.26%.
- **[Margin Trading](../m/margin_trading.md) Fees**: A [fee](../f/fee.md) is charged whenever borrowed funds are used, as well as a [rollover](../r/rollover.md) [fee](../f/fee.md) for positions held overnight.
- **[Futures](../f/futures.md) Trading Fees**: Maker fees [range](../r/range.md) from 0.02% to 0.05%, and taker fees [range](../r/range.md) from 0.04% to 0.075%.

### User Interface

Kraken has developed [multiple](../m/multiple.md) interfaces to cater to different user needs:

- **Kraken Web Platform**../r/robust.md) and intuitive trading experience with various tools and charts.
- **Kraken Pro**: This is a more advanced [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) designed for professional traders, [offering](../o/offering.md) detailed charts, [order book](../o/order_book.md) data, and advanced [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md).
- **Mobile Apps**: Kraken provides both a standard app and a Pro version for mobile users, ensuring that traders can manage their portfolios on the go.

### Regulatory Compliance

Kraken is fully compliant with numerous regulatory requirements in various jurisdictions. Some of its regulatory licenses and affiliations include:

- **Financial Services Provider (FSP)**: Registered as a Financial Services Provider in New Zealand.
- **Crypto Facilities**: A regulated [futures market](../f/futures_market.md) based in the UK.
- **Fiat [Currency](../c/currency.md) Support**: Users can [deposit](../d/deposit.md) and withdraw in several fiat currencies, such as USD, EUR, CAD, and JPY. Kraken also supports [bank](../b/bank.md) transfers and SWIFT payments.

### API for Algorithmic Trading

For users interested in [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md), Kraken offers a comprehensive API suite. This includes both REST and WebSocket APIs, providing access to real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data, trading functionalities, and account management.

- **REST API**: Suitable for accessing historical data, placing trades, and managing accounts.
- **WebSocket API**: Provides [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md), including [order book](../o/order_book.md) updates, [trade](../t/trade.md) executions, and ticker information.

The APIs are well-documented, and Kraken provides sample code and libraries to help developers integrate them into their [trading algorithms](../t/trading_algorithms.md).

### Customer Support

- **Help Centre**: Extensive documentation and FAQs to help users troubleshoot common issues.
- **Support Tickets**: Users can submit support tickets for more complicated issues, receiving responses typically within 24 hours.
- **Live Chat**: Available to assist users with urgent issues.

### Real-World Use Cases

Kraken serves a diverse [range](../r/range.md) of users, including retail investors, institutional investors, and high-frequency traders. Some real-world use cases include:

- **Hedging**: Traders can use Kraken [Futures](../f/futures.md) to [hedge](../h/hedge.md) against price [volatility](../v/volatility.md) in their cryptocurrency [holdings](../h/holdings.md).
- **[Portfolio Diversification](../p/portfolio_diversification.md)**: Investors can diversify their portfolios by trading a variety of cryptocurrencies offered on Kraken.
- **[Margin Trading](../m/margin_trading.md)**: Enables traders to maximize their gains (and losses) by leveraging their positions.
- **Staking**: Allows long-term holders to earn rewards on their [holdings](../h/holdings.md).
