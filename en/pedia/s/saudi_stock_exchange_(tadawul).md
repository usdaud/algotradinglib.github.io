# Saudi Stock Exchange (Tadawul)

The Saudi Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (Tadawul) is the sole entity authorized in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to act as the securities [exchange](../e/exchange.md), and it falls under the oversight of the [Capital](../c/capital.md) [Market](../m/market.md) Authority (CMA). Established in 2007, Tadawul is a key player in the [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md) of the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region. Tadawul provides a platform for buying, selling, and trading of equities, bonds, [sukuk](../s/sukuk.md), mutual funds, and [exchange](../e/exchange.md)-traded funds (ETFs). It aims to support the development of the Saudi [capital](../c/capital.md) [market](../m/market.md) in line with the objectives set out in the Kingdom's Vision 2030.

## Market Structure

### Equities
Equities or [stocks](../s/stock.md) represent ownership in companies [listed](../l/listed.md) on Tadawul. Investors can buy or sell [shares](../s/shares.md) through licensed brokers. Some prominent companies [listed](../l/listed.md) include Saudi Aramco, Saudi Basic Industries [Corporation](../c/corporation.md) (SABIC), and Al Rajhi [Bank](../b/bank.md).

### Sukuk and Bonds
Tadawul provides a platform for trading Sharia-compliant [sukuk](../s/sukuk.md) and conventional bonds, which [offer](../o/offer.md) investment opportunities with varying [risk](../r/risk.md) and [return](../r/return.md) profiles.

### ETFs
ETFs or [Exchange](../e/exchange.md)-Traded Funds are investment funds traded on the stock [exchange](../e/exchange.md), designed to track the performance of an [index](../i/index_instrument.md), [commodity](../c/commodity.md), or a basket of assets. They [offer](../o/offer.md) diversified exposure and are a popular [investment vehicle](../i/investment_vehicle.md).

### Mutual Funds
Mutual funds are investment programs funded by shareholders that [trade](../t/trade.md) in diversified [holdings](../h/holdings.md) and are professionally managed. Tadawul lists various mutual funds managed by licensed [fund](../f/fund.md) managers.

## Market Indices

### TASI (Tadawul All Share Index)
The TASI is the main stock [market index](../m/market_index.md), representing the performance of all [listed](../l/listed.md) companies on Tadawul. It serves as a [benchmark](../b/benchmark.md) for the overall health and performance of the [equity market](../e/equity_market.md).

### Nomu - Parallel Market
Nomu is an alternative [equity market](../e/equity_market.md) with lighter [listing requirements](../l/listing_requirements.md), aimed at [offering](../o/offering.md) growth opportunities to smaller companies and providing investors with more investment [options](../o/options.md).

## Trading Platform

Tadawul employs sophisticated trading technology to ensure seamless [transaction execution](../t/transaction_execution.md), [market](../m/market.md) [transparency](../t/transparency.md), and integrity. It has introduced several technology-driven solutions to improve trading [efficiency](../e/efficiency.md).

### X-Stream INET
Tadawul uses X-Stream INET, a high-performance [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) provided by [Nasdaq](../n/nasdaq.md), which supports [robust](../r/robust.md) [market](../m/market.md) operations with low latency and high [throughput](../t/throughput.md).

### Market Information System (MIS)
The [Market](../m/market.md) Information System provides real-time data and analytics to [market](../m/market.md) participants, facilitating informed decision-making.

## Regulatory Framework

### Capital Market Authority (CMA)
The CMA oversees the functioning of Tadawul to ensure [market](../m/market.md) integrity and [investor](../i/investor.md) protection. It sets the regulatory framework, enforces [market](../m/market.md) rules, and oversees compliance.

### Market Surveillance
Tadawul employs advanced [market](../m/market.md) surveillance systems to detect and prevent [market manipulation](../m/market_manipulation.md), [insider trading](../i/insider.md), and other fraudulent activities. It collaborates with the CMA to enforce [market](../m/market.md) discipline.

## Vision 2030 and Tadawul

Saudi Vision 2030 is a strategic framework aimed at diversifying the Saudi [economy](../e/economy.md) and reducing its dependence on oil. As part of Vision 2030, Tadawul plays a crucial role in developing the [capital](../c/capital.md) [market](../m/market.md) by:

### Promoting IPOs
Encouraging more companies to go public to drive [capital](../c/capital.md) [market](../m/market.md) growth and diversify investment opportunities.

### Developing New Financial Products
Introducing new financial instruments, such as REITs ([Real Estate](../r/real_estate.md) Investment Trusts) and [Derivatives](../d/derivatives.md), to attract a broader [range](../r/range.md) of investors.

### Enhancing Market Participation
Improving accessibility for foreign investors through initiatives like the Qualified Foreign [Investor](../i/investor.md) (QFI) program, allowing them to [trade](../t/trade.md) selected securities.

### Sustainability and Governance
Implementing high standards for Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria among [listed](../l/listed.md) companies to attract global sustainable investment inflows.

### Financial Education
Promoting [financial literacy](../f/financial_literacy.md) among the populace to increase individual participation in the [stock market](../s/stock_market.md).

## Statistics

### Market Capitalization
As of 2023, the [market capitalization](../m/market_capitalization.md) of Tadawul exceeds SAR 10 trillion (approximately USD 2.7 trillion), making it one of the largest stock exchanges in the world.

### Daily Trading Volume
The average daily trading [volume](../v/volume.md) on Tadawul is around SAR 10 billion (approximately USD 2.7 billion).

### Number of Listed Companies
Over 200 companies are [listed](../l/listed.md) on Tadawul, spanning various sectors, including financial, industrial, healthcare, and consumer services.

### Foreign Ownership
The foreign ownership in the Saudi [stock market](../s/stock_market.md) has been steadily increasing, facilitated by initiatives aimed at easing investment restrictions.
