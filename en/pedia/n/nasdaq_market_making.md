# NASDAQ Market Making

[NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md) [market](../m/market.md) making refers to the role and activities of [market](../m/market.md) makers on the [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md) [stock market](../s/stock_market.md). [Market](../m/market.md) makers are crucial participants in the [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md) as they provide [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) by being ready to buy and sell securities at publicly quoted prices. On [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md), [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [market](../m/market.md) makers might be involved in trading a single [security](../s/security.md), and their competition can lead to tighter [bid](../b/bid.md)-ask [spreads](../s/spreads.md) and more efficient [price discovery](../p/price_discovery.md). This documentation [will](../w/will.md) delve into the objectives, functions, regulations, and impacts of [market](../m/market.md) making on [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md).

### Objectives of Market Makers

1. **[Liquidity Provision](../l/liquidity_provision.md)**: [Market](../m/market.md) makers are committed to providing [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) in the securities they cover. They do this by maintaining buy and sell limit orders in the [market](../m/market.md), which helps ensure that investors can execute trades promptly.

2. **Reducing [Volatility](../v/volatility.md)**: By providing [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), [market](../m/market.md) makers help reduce [volatility](../v/volatility.md) in the [market](../m/market.md). They do this by smoothing out fluctuations in [supply](../s/supply.md) and [demand](../d/demand.md) for securities.

3. **Efficient [Price Discovery](../p/price_discovery.md)**: [Market](../m/market.md) makers contribute to the efficient discovery of stock prices. By continuously quoting prices at which they are willing to buy and sell, they help in determining the fair [market price](../m/market_price.md) of a [security](../s/security.md) based on real-time [demand](../d/demand.md) and [supply](../s/supply.md) conditions.

### Functions of Market Makers

- **[Quote](../q/quote.md) Management**: [Market](../m/market.md) makers are required to continuously display buy ([bid](../b/bid.md)) and sell (ask) quotations. These quotes need to be competitive to attract [order](../o/order.md) flow.

- **[Order](../o/order.md) [Execution](../e/execution.md)**: [Market](../m/market.md) makers execute orders from clients or their own accounts. They might fill these orders from their [inventory](../i/inventory.md) or by finding a [counterparty](../c/counterparty.md) on the opposite side of the [trade](../t/trade.md).

- **[Inventory Management](../i/inventory_management.md)**: Managing [inventory](../i/inventory.md) is a critical function of [market](../m/market.md) makers. They balance desired positions to avoid significant exposures to [market risk](../m/market_risk.md), while being ready to fulfill [market](../m/market.md) demands.

- **[Broker-Dealer](../b/broker-dealer.md) Activities**: Some [market](../m/market.md) makers might also act as brokers, executing trades on behalf of clients for a [commission](../c/commission.md) or [fee](../f/fee.md).

### Regulation of Market Makers

[Market](../m/market.md) makers are subject to stringent regulations designed to ensure the integrity and fairness of the [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md). On [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md), [market maker](../m/market_maker.md) activities are regulated by the Financial [Industry](../i/industry.md) Regulatory Authority ([FINRA](../f/finra.md)) and the Securities and [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) [Commission](../c/commission.md) (SEC). Key regulations include:

- **Regulation NMS (National [Market](../m/market.md) System)**: Mandates that [market](../m/market.md) makers ensure a high degree of competition and [transparency](../t/transparency.md) in trading activities.

- **[FINRA](../f/finra.md) Rules**: [Market](../m/market.md) makers must adhere to various [FINRA](../f/finra.md) rules, such as maintaining fair and orderly markets, providing accurate and timely [trade](../t/trade.md) reports, and avoiding conflicts of [interest](../i/interest.md).

- **SEC Rule 15c3-5**: This rule requires [market](../m/market.md) makers to implement [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) controls and supervisory procedures to limit [financial exposure](../f/financial_exposure.md).

### Technology in Market Making

[Market](../m/market.md) making on [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md) has evolved significantly with technology. High-frequency trading (HFT) and advanced algorithms are now common in [market](../m/market.md) making. These technologies enable [market](../m/market.md) makers to react to [market](../m/market.md) conditions in microseconds, analyze vast amounts of data, and execute complex [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) with high precision.

- **[Algorithmic Trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md)**: [Algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) systems enable [market](../m/market.md) makers to analyze [market](../m/market.md) data, detect trading opportunities, and execute trades at superior speed and [efficiency](../e/efficiency.md).

- **High-Frequency Trading**: HFT leverages sophisticated algorithms and state-of-the-art technology to execute a large number of orders at extremely high speeds. [Market](../m/market.md) makers using HFT can benefit from small price discrepancies in highly [liquid](../l/liquid.md) securities.

### Impact on NASDAQ

- **[Liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) and [Volume](../v/volume.md)**: [Market](../m/market.md) makers contribute significantly to the [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) of securities on [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md), facilitating higher trading volumes.

- **Spread Reduction**: The involvement of [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [market](../m/market.md) makers often results in narrower [bid](../b/bid.md)-ask [spreads](../s/spreads.md) due to competition, benefiting investors with lower [transaction costs](../t/transaction_costs.md).

- **[Market](../m/market.md) Stability**: By providing a continuous [market](../m/market.md) for securities, [market](../m/market.md) makers help stabilize price movements and maintain orderly trading conditions.

### Major Market Makers on NASDAQ

Some prominent [market](../m/market.md) makers on [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md) include:

- **Citadel Securities**: Known for its dominant role in [equity](../e/equity.md) and [options market making](../o/options_market_making.md).

- **Virtu Financial**: One of the largest electronic [market](../m/market.md) makers globally, active across numerous [asset](../a/asset.md) classes.

- **Jane Street**: Renowned for [quantitative research](../q/quantitative_research.md)-driven trading and [market](../m/market.md) making across various financial instruments.

### Conclusion

[NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md) [market](../m/market.md) making is instrumental in maintaining [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), price stability, and efficient operation of the [exchange](../e/exchange.md). With the shift towards electronic and [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md), [market](../m/market.md) makers have become even more sophisticated in their strategies and technology, ensuring that the [market](../m/market.md) functions smoothly and effectively for all participants.
