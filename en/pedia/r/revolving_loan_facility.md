# Revolving Loan Facility

A revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) [facility](../f/facility.md) is a type of [credit](../c/credit.md) issued by a financial institution that provides the borrower with the flexibility to draw down, repay, and redrawn loans advanced to it. It is similar to a [credit card](../c/credit_card.md) in terms of usage: the borrower can access funds up to a certain [credit limit](../c/credit_limit.md), repay these funds, and borrow again as needed. Revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) facilities are frequently used by companies to manage their working [capital](../c/capital.md) needs because they [offer](../o/offer.md) a readily available source of funds on an ongoing [basis](../b/basis.md).

Note: LIBOR publication ended for most tenors after 2023, and markets shifted to risk-free reference rates such as SOFR (USD), SONIA (GBP), and ESTR (EUR).

## Key Features of Revolving Loan Facility

- **[Credit Limit](../c/credit_limit.md)**: This is the maximum amount of [money](../m/money.md) that the borrower can access at any given time. The [credit limit](../c/credit_limit.md) is set by the [lender](../l/lender.md) and can be based on the borrowerâ€™s [creditworthiness](../c/creditworthiness.md), [business](../b/business.md) profile, and [financial health](../f/financial_health.md).
- **[Drawdown](../d/drawdown.md)**: The borrower can draw down funds as necessary, meaning they can take out [money](../m/money.md) whenever needed, up to the [credit limit](../c/credit_limit.md).
- **[Repayment](../r/repayment.md)**: The borrower is required to make regular payments which could be [interest](../i/interest.md)-only or include both [interest](../i/interest.md) and [principal](../p/principal.md). If the borrower repays more than the minimum requirement, they can re-borrow that amount again.
- **[Interest](../i/interest.md) Rates**: [Interest](../i/interest.md) on a revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) [facility](../f/facility.md) is typically variable and often linked to a [benchmark](../b/benchmark.md) rate such as the LIBOR or [prime rate](../p/prime_rate.md).
- **Fees**: These may include commitment fees, which are charged on the unused portion of the [facility](../f/facility.md), and/or [origination](../o/origination.md) fees, which cover the administrative costs of setting up the [loan](../l/loan.md).

## Types of Revolving Loan Facilities

1. **Committed Revolving [Loan](../l/loan.md) [Facility](../f/facility.md)**: In this type, the [lender](../l/lender.md) is obligated to provide funds up to the specified limit, and the borrower pays a [fee](../f/fee.md) on the unused portion. This type offers reliability and stability but could be more expensive due to fees.
2. **Uncommitted Revolving [Loan](../l/loan.md) [Facility](../f/facility.md)**: Here, the [lender](../l/lender.md) is not obligated to provide funds and can decline requests for drawdowns. These are generally less expensive but [offer](../o/offer.md) less certainty for the borrower.
3. **Revolving [Letter of Credit](../l/letter_of_credit.md)**: This is a [financial instrument](../f/financial_instrument.md) that provides the [beneficiary](../b/beneficiary.md) with the ability to draw funds as required. It is generally used in international [trade](../t/trade.md) for securing transactions.

## Advantages of Revolving Loan Facilities

- **Flexibility**: Borrowers can access funds as needed and repay them as their [cash flow](../c/cash_flow.md) allows.
- **Cost-Effective**: [Interest](../i/interest.md) is only paid on the funds actually borrowed, not on the entire [credit limit](../c/credit_limit.md).
- **Convenience**: Easy to manage and access compared to obtaining [multiple](../m/multiple.md) short-term loans.
- **Supports [Cash Flow](../c/cash_flow.md) Management**: Ideal for managing seasonal fluctuations in [revenue](../r/revenue.md) and expenses.

## Disadvantages of Revolving Loan Facilities

- **[Interest Rate Risk](../i/interest_rate_risk.md)**: Variable [interest](../i/interest.md) rates mean that the borrowing costs can increase unexpectedly.
- **Fees**: Commitment fees on unused portions can add to the cost of borrowing.
- **[Creditworthiness](../c/creditworthiness.md) Requirements**: Lenders often require strong [credit](../c/credit.md) past performance and [financial health](../f/financial_health.md) to approve a revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) [facility](../f/facility.md).
- **Strict Covenants**: Borrowers might face restrictive covenants, including maintaining certain [financial ratios](../f/financial_ratios.md) and limits on additional borrowing.

## Usage in Companies

Revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) facilities are commonly used by businesses as part of their [working capital management](../w/working_capital_management.md). For instance, a retail [business](../b/business.md) might use a revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) [facility](../f/facility.md) to cover the costs of [inventory](../i/inventory.md) purchases before the peak shopping season, and then repay the [loan](../l/loan.md) with the [revenue](../r/revenue.md) generated during that period.

## Examples and Case Studies

- **General Electric (GE)**: General Electric often utilizes [revolving credit](../r/revolving_credit.md) facilities to meet short-term [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) requirements and manage working [capital](../c/capital.md) effectively. Without such facilities, GE would find it challenging to maintain the flexibility needed in its diverse and often dynamically changing operational environment.
- **JP Morgan Chase**: JP Morgan offers revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) facilities as part of its corporate banking services, providing both committed and uncommitted revolving lines of [credit](../c/credit.md) to clients.

- **Tech Startups**: Startups in the tech [industry](../i/industry.md) frequently use revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) facilities to manage the volatile [cash flow](../c/cash_flow.md) associated with R&D expenses and [capital](../c/capital.md) investments. The flexibility allows them to draw funds when [venture capital](../v/venture_capital.md) infusions are delayed or seasonal [revenue](../r/revenue.md) dips occur.

## Financial and Legal Considerations

When entering into a revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) [facility](../f/facility.md) agreement, companies should conduct thorough [due diligence](../d/due_diligence.md) to understand all terms and conditions. Legal counsel is often advisable to negotiate terms that can [offer](../o/offer.md) some degree of protection against adverse conditions, such as sudden rate hikes or overly restrictive covenants. Financial considerations should include assessing the total cost of borrowing, including [interest](../i/interest.md) and fees.

## Conclusion

A revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) [facility](../f/facility.md) offers an effective solution for managing short-term [capital](../c/capital.md) needs, providing flexibility, and ensuring [business](../b/business.md) continuity. companies can [leverage](../l/leverage.md) this [facility](../f/facility.md) to manage fluctuations in [cash flow](../c/cash_flow.md) and [capitalize](../c/capitalize.md) on [business](../b/business.md) opportunities without the need to go through a lengthy [loan](../l/loan.md) approval process each time funds are required. For businesses with seasonal cycles or variable operating needs, this financial tool can be a critical part of their financial strategy. However, it is essential to weigh the benefits against the costs and potential risks, such as [interest rate](../i/interest_rate.md) fluctuations and fees.

In sum, revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) facilities represent a versatile borrowing option, making them a popular choice among businesses worldwide. By understanding the structure, benefits, and drawbacks, companies can make informed decisions that align with their financial goals and operational needs.

Lastly, having strong financial metrics and maintaining good relationships with lenders can often lead to more favorable terms, thereby enhancing the [utility](../u/utility.md) and cost-effectiveness of the revolving [loan](../l/loan.md) [facility](../f/facility.md). As the [business](../b/business.md) landscape evolves, such adaptive financial instruments are likely to remain integral to many companies' [financial planning](../f/financial_planning.md) and [risk management frameworks](../r/risk_management_frameworks.md).
