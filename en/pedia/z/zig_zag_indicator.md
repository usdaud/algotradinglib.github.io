# Zig Zag Indicator

The Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) is a popular [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md) tool used by traders to identify changes in a [security](../s/security.md)’s [trend](../t/trend.md), filtering out minor price fluctuations and focusing on significant movements. This helps traders to visualize trends, measure retracements, and detect [reversal](../r/reversal.md) points for making informed trading decisions. Unlike other indicators that may provide continuous lines or histograms, the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) provides a series of straight lines that encompass only the most critical price changes.

## How it Works

The Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) connects peaks and troughs on a price chart, filtering out smaller price movements by a specified percentage threshold. By doing so, it 'cleans' the [price action](../p/price_action.md) and highlights only the significant movements. This makes it particularly useful for:

1. **Identifying price trends:** By filtering out [noise](../n/noise.md), the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) clearly shows whether the [market](../m/market.md) is trending upwards, downwards, or sideways.
2. **Measuring [market](../m/market.md) waves:** Traders can determine the extent of the [market](../m/market.md)'s waves and identify potential [reversal](../r/reversal.md) points, which can be crucial for strategy development.
3. **[Retracement](../r/retracement.md) analysis:** It helps in identifying common [retracement](../r/retracement.md) levels, such as 38.2%, 50%, and 61.8%, which are essential for [Fibonacci retracement](../f/fibonacci_retracement.md) analysis.

## Calculation

The Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) is calculated by using the following steps:

1. Determine the significant price changes by setting a percentage threshold. This threshold is typically set between 5% to 20%, but traders can adjust it according to their [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
2. The [indicator](../i/indicator.md) starts plotting from the first peak or [trough](../t/trough.md) found on the chart.
3. It continues to plot a straight line to the next significant high or low that exceeds the specified threshold.
4. Repeat the above steps, continuing to connect significant highs and lows on the chart.

## Application in Trading Strategies

### Trend Following

The Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) is highly effective in identifying trends and confirming other [trend](../t/trend.md)-following indicators. For instance, a [trader](../t/trader.md) might use it in conjunction with moving averages or the [Average Directional Index](../a/average_directional_index_(adx).md) (ADX) to confirm a [trend](../t/trend.md).

### Elliott Wave Theory

The [indicator](../i/indicator.md) is also used to identify the waves in [Elliott Wave Theory](../e/elliott_wave_theory.md), a form of [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md) that asserts [market](../m/market.md) prices unfold in specific patterns known as waves. The Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) helps traders see these waves more clearly.

### Fibonacci Retracements

By identifying significant peaks and troughs, the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) facilitates the use of Fibonacci retracements. Traders often draw Fibonacci levels on a Zig Zag line to identify potential [support and resistance](../s/support_and_resistance.md) levels.

### Combined with Other Indicators

The Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) can be used alongside other [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicator.md) to build more [robust](../r/robust.md) [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md). For example:

- **[Relative Strength](../r/relative_strength.md) [Index](../i/index_instrument.md) (RSI):** When the Zig Zag line indicates a potential [reversal](../r/reversal.md), checking the RSI can confirm whether the [security](../s/security.md) is [overbought](../o/overbought.md) or [oversold](../o/oversold.md).
- **Moving Averages:** To get a clearer picture of the [trend](../t/trend.md), combining the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) with moving averages can [offer](../o/offer.md) precise entry and exit points.

### Automated Trading Systems (Algorithmic Trading)

In [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md), the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) can be used to automate entry and exit points based on significant price movements. It can be an essential part of a trading algorithm that looks for specific patterns such as double tops, double bottoms, or head and shoulders.

## Limitations

While the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) is helpful in filtering out [noise](../n/noise.md), it has its drawbacks:

- **[Lagging Indicator](../l/lagging_indicator.md):** It is a [lagging indicator](../l/lagging_indicator.md) that needs significant price movement to redraw itself. This means that it can often provide signals after the fact.
- **Does Not Predict Future Trends:** The Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) does not predict future [market](../m/market.md) trends but rather helps in analyzing previous price movements.
- **Parameter Sensitivity:** The effectiveness of the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) greatly depends on the chosen percentage threshold. A too-large threshold may filter out important price movements, while a too-small threshold may introduce [noise](../n/noise.md).

## How to Use on Trading Platforms

Most trading platforms [offer](../o/offer.md) the Zig Zag [indicator](../i/indicator.md) as a part of their toolkit. Here’s how you can set it up on some popular platforms:

### MetaTrader 4 (MT4)

1. [Open](../o/open.md) your MT4 platform.
2. From the 'Insert' menu, go to 'Indicators.'
3. Select 'Custom' and then choose 'ZigZag.'
4. Adjust the setting parameters like Depth, Deviation, and Backstep according to your strategy.

### TradingView

1. [Open](../o/open.md) the [TradingView](../t/tradingview.md) chart.
2. Click on the 'Indicators' tab at the top.
3. Search for 'Zig Zag' and click to add it to your chart.
4. Customize the settings like 'Deviation' and 'Depth' as per your requirements.

### NinjaTrader

1. [Open](../o/open.md) [NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md) and [load](../l/load.md) your desired chart.
2. Right-click on the chart and select 'Indicators.'
3. Find 'Zig Zag' in the list and double-click to add.
4. Adjust the settings for parameters like 'Deviation,' 'Depth,' and 'Backstep.'
