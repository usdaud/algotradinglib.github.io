# XT

Founded in 2018, XT is a comprehensive cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) platform [offering](../o/offering.md) a wide array of services encompassing OTC trading, spot trading, [futures](../f/futures.md) trading, [margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md), and staking. XT aims to provide users with a secure, transparent, and efficient trading experience. It has quickly grown to become one of the more significant players in the cryptocurrency trading [industry](../i/industry.md). This article delves into the various aspects of XT, including its services, features, [security](../s/security.md) measures, and overall impact on the crypto [market](../m/market.md).

## Origin and Mission

XT was founded with the mission of simplifying and democratizing access to cryptocurrencies. By providing a user-friendly platform with a diverse [range](../r/range.md) of trading [options](../o/options.md), XT seeks to cater to both novice and experienced traders. The platform prioritizes [security](../s/security.md), [transparency](../t/transparency.md), and user satisfaction, ensuring that users have a reliable and comprehensive trading experience.

## Core Services

### Spot Trading

Spot trading is the most fundamental service offered by XT. It involves the buying and selling of cryptocurrencies for immediate settlement. XT supports an extensive list of cryptocurrencies, allowing users to [trade](../t/trade.md) a [wide variety](../w/wide_variety.md) of digital assets.

### Futures Trading

[Futures](../f/futures.md) trading involves the trading of contracts that obligate the purchase or [sale](../s/sale.md) of an [asset](../a/asset.md) at a predetermined future date and price. XT offers [futures](../f/futures.md) trading with various [leverage](../l/leverage.md) [options](../o/options.md), enabling traders to maximize their potential returns while managing [risk](../r/risk.md).

### Margin Trading

[Margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md) allows users to borrow funds to [trade](../t/trade.md) larger positions than they could with their [capital](../c/capital.md) alone. XT provides [margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md) with different [leverage ratios](../l/leverage_ratios.md), giving traders the flexibility to amplify their gains or losses.

### OTC Trading

[Over-the-Counter (OTC) trading](../o/over-the-counter_(otc)_trading.md) is designed for large-[volume](../v/volume.md) traders who prefer to execute trades directly with a [counterparty](../c/counterparty.md) rather than through the public [order book](../o/order_book.md). XT's OTC desk offers personalized service and competitive pricing for high-[volume](../v/volume.md) trades.

### Staking

XT also provides staking services, allowing users to earn rewards by participating in the proof-of-stake (PoS) consensus mechanism of supported cryptocurrencies. This service offers users a way to earn [passive income](../p/passive_income.md) by simply holding and staking their assets on the platform.

## Security Measures

### Cold Storage

The majority of XT's user funds are stored in cold wallets, which are offline storage solutions that are not connected to the internet. This protects the funds from potential cyber-attacks and hacking attempts.

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

XT implements two-[factor](../f/factor.md) authentication (2FA) to add an extra layer of [security](../s/security.md) to user accounts. This requires users to verify their identity using a secondary method (such as a mobile app or SMS) in addition to their password.

### Regular Security Audits

The platform undergoes regular [security](../s/security.md) audits and vulnerability assessments to ensure that its systems and processes are secure and up to date. Any identified vulnerabilities are addressed promptly to maintain the integrity of the platform.

### Insurance Fund

XT has established an [insurance](../i/insurance.md) [fund](../f/fund.md) to protect users' assets in the event of unforeseen incidents, such as hacking or [security](../s/security.md) breaches. This [fund](../f/fund.md) provides an additional layer of [security](../s/security.md) and reassurance for users.
